Welcome to Forbella Business Journal

At the Forbella Business Journal, our mission is to advance the understanding and practice of business, entrepreneurship, and management in Africa. We aim to provide a platform for academics, practitioners, and policymakers to share their insights, research findings, and innovative ideas.

Our journal features a wide range of articles, from theoretical research to practical case studies, all aimed at fostering a deeper understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities within the African business landscape.
